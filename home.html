About WE School for Applied Technology

We School for Applied Technology prepares students for the future of technology through hands-on training and modern education. In partnership with WE Telecom Egypt and the Ministry of Education, the school equips learners with the skills and experience needed to succeed in today’s digital world. With a strong focus on information technology, networks, cyber security, and telecommunications, students gain real-world experience inside specialized labs and through practical projects. The school constantly updates its programs to match the high standards of the tech industry and the fast-paced development of global digital transformation.
